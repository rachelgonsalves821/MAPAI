01  Brand Overview
What is Mapai?
Mapai is an AI-native discovery platform that replaces the generic map experience with a personal co-pilot. It learns who you are, predicts what you want, and gets you there — all from one conversational interface. Think less Google Maps, more knowledgeable local friend.

Brand Mission

Brand Personality


Target Audience
Gen Z and Millennial urban dwellers (18–35), primarily in Boston. They are digital-first, socially aware, value authenticity over polish, and are deeply skeptical of algorithmic feed fatigue. They want tools that feel smart — not creepy.
02  Logo & Wordmark
Naming Convention
The brand name is always written as: mapai (all lowercase in UI copy) or MAPAI in display/wordmark contexts. Never Map AI (two words) or MapAI (camel case).

Logomark Concept

Logo Variations

Clear Space
Maintain a minimum clear space of 1× the cap-height of the wordmark on all four sides. Never place the logo on a busy photographic background without a translucent scrim layer (black at 30–40% opacity minimum).

04  Typography
Primary Typefaces: Clash Display + Cabinet Grotesk
Mapai uses a two-typeface system: Clash Display for all headings, display text, and brand moments; Cabinet Grotesk for all body copy, UI labels, captions, and data. Together they create a premium app feeling — bold and geometric at the top, clean and readable everywhere else. Think Notion meets fintech. On iOS, San Francisco is the system fallback; on Android, Roboto.

05  Spacing & Layout
Grid System
Mapai uses a 4px base unit grid. All spacing, padding, margin, and sizing values must be multiples of 4px. Target device: 390px wide (iPhone 14 / 15 base model) as canonical design size.


Safe Zones & Margins
Horizontal screen margin: 16px (mobile). Content max-width for list views: 100% of viewport minus margins. Bottom safe area: always account for home indicator (34px on iPhone X+). Status bar: 44–54px top. Bottom navigation height: 56px + safe area inset.

Corner Radius System
06  Core Components
Bottom Navigation Bar
5 tabs: Map, Discover (Chat), Search, Social, Profile. Active tab: Electric Blue icon + filled dot indicator. Inactive: Stone grey icon. Height: 56px + safe area inset. Background: white with 12px blur backdrop. No border line — use soft box shadow (0px -1px 0px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)).

Chat Input Bar (Persistent)
Floating pill at bottom of map screen. Background: white. Border: 1px Cloud (#D1D5DB). Shadow: 0 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.10). Inner padding: 12px 16px. Left: microphone icon (Stone). Right: send button (Electric Blue filled circle, 36px). Placeholder text: 'Where to?' — Body Default, Stone. On focus: border becomes Electric Blue 2px.

Primary Button
Background: #0558E8. Text: white, Cabinet Grotesk SemiBold 16px. Height: 52px. Corner radius: 12px. Pressed: #0440B8 background. Disabled: #D1D5DB background, Stone text. Min tap target: 44×44px. Icon: 20px, 8px gap from text. Never use ALL CAPS for button labels.

Secondary Button
Background: white or transparent. Border: 1.5px #0558E8. Text: #0558E8, Cabinet Grotesk SemiBold 16px. Height: 52px. Corner radius: 12px. Pressed: #E0ECFF background fill.

Place Card (Map Pin Card)
Triggered on tap of a map pin. Bottom sheet mini-card, 16px horizontal margin. White background, 12px corner radius, 16px shadow. Structure: [photo thumbnail 56×56px, 8px radius] + [name H3 + category tag + match score badge] + [two CTAs: 'Details' secondary | 'Navigate' primary]. Swipe down to dismiss.

Match Score Badge
Round badge displaying score 0–100. Size: 36×36px. Background: Electric Blue. Text: Clash Display Bold 14px white tabular. For high match (70+): solid blue. Medium (40–69): lighter blue (#1E7FFF). Low (<40): Stone grey. Always pair score with a one-line reasoning string.

Filter Chip
Pill shape (999px radius). Height: 32px. Padding: 8px 14px. Inactive: white background, 1px Cloud border, Ink text, Stone icon. Active: Electric Blue background, white text, white icon. Font: Cabinet Grotesk Medium 13px. Never truncate chip text — reduce font size to 11px minimum before truncating.

Chat Bubble — AI Response
Left aligned. Background: #F0F6FF (Mist). Left border: 3px solid #7C3AED (AI Violet). Corner radius: 0px top-left, 12px all other corners. Padding: 12px 14px. Text: Body Default, Ink. AI icon: 20px violet glyph top-left, 8px above bubble.

Chat Bubble — User Message
Right aligned. Background: #0558E8 (Electric Blue). Corner radius: 12px top-left, 0px bottom-right, 12px all others. Padding: 12px 14px. Text: Body Default, white.

Bottom Sheet / Drawer
Full-width. Top corners: 24px radius. Background: white. Drag handle: 36×4px, Stone, centred, 8px from top. Default collapsed height: 25% viewport. Expanded: 90% viewport. Transition: spring (damping 0.7, stiffness 300). Always has a scrim overlay (black 30% opacity) on the map beneath when expanded.

Toast / Snackbar
Appears at bottom above nav bar. Background: #111827 (Ink). Text: white, Body Small, Cabinet Grotesk Regular. Icon: 16px coloured (success/error/info). Auto-dismiss: 3s. Corner radius: 12px. Max width: screen width minus 32px margins. One action button max (e.g., 'Undo') in Electric Blue.

Loading States
Skeleton screens: use #E5EEFF shimmer rectangles matching the exact shape of the content loading. Never use spinners in place of content. For AI typing: animated three-dot pulse in AI Violet. For map pins loading: fade-in stagger with 40ms delay per pin.
07  Iconography
Icon set: Lucide Icons. Grid: 24×24px. Stroke weight: 1.5px. Line cap: round. Line join: round. Never filled icons (exception: tab bar active states use filled glyph). Minimum touch target for any tappable icon: 44×44px (add invisible padding).

Icon Colour Rules

Custom Icon Requirements
The following icons are custom to Mapai and must be designed from scratch matching the Lucide stroke style: Map Pin (personalised, with score ring), Loyalty Pin (pin with gold ring), Friend Pin (pin with avatar cutout), AI Copilot (abstract neural/orbit glyph), Match Score Ring (donut progress ring).
08  Motion & Animation
Motion is a core part of the Mapai experience. Every animation should feel like it has physical weight and intent — not decorative. Use motion to confirm actions, guide attention, and reinforce the app's intelligence.


Animation Principles
09  Photography & Media
Place Photography
All place photos sourced from Google Places API or Instagram Graph API. Apply a subtle 5% opacity Electric Blue colour wash on hover/focus state of photo thumbnails to reinforce brand. Photos must always be cropped to 16:9 for hero cards, 1:1 for thumbnail pins, and 4:3 for carousel cards.

Photo Overlay
When overlaying text on photos (e.g., place name on hero image), apply a linear gradient scrim from transparent (top) to rgba(0,0,0,0.65) (bottom). Never use a flat solid overlay. Minimum text contrast on scrim: 4.5:1.

Illustration Style
Onboarding, empty states, and error screens may use custom illustration. Style: clean vector, thin strokes, Electric Blue primary palette with Mint and Violet accents. Characters (if any): diverse, Gen-Z coded, minimal facial detail. No skeuomorphic or realistic illustration styles.

Social Signal Quotes
When surfacing Reddit / Instagram / Google Review quotes on place detail cards: display as a pull-quote block with a #F0F6FF background, left border in the source brand colour (Reddit: orange, Google: blue, Instagram: gradient via icon), and source icon at 16px. Truncate at 120 characters with 'Read more' expansion.
10  Screen Design Specifications
Home / Map Screen
Background: map canvas (MapKit iOS / Google Maps Android, minimalist style — muted greens and greys, Electric Blue roads). Overlays: bottom chat pill (always visible), top-left neighbourhood chip, top-right avatar. Filter chips scroll horizontally below the top bar. Bottom sheet collapsed by default. Map pins animate in with 40ms stagger on load.

Chat / Discovery Screen
Map fades to 30% opacity on chat expand — geographic context preserved. Chat thread scrolls upward. AI bubbles left with Violet left border, user bubbles right in Electric Blue. Place result cards scroll horizontally inside the chat thread — not below it. Voice input shows animated waveform in Electric Blue during active listening.

Place Detail Screen
Full-screen modal with 24px top corner radius and drag handle. Photo carousel at top (16:9, full bleed). Sticky header on scroll: place name + 2 CTA buttons. Match score ring: 64px donut ring in Electric Blue, score number inside, reasoning text below in Body Small Stone. Social signals section with source-branded quote cards. Tab row: Overview / Reviews / Map.

Transport Comparison Screen
Map preview at top 40% of screen showing route polyline (colour-coded by selected mode). Cards below in a single scroll column. Each card: mode icon (24px), transport name, ETA in H2, cost in H2, secondary detail in Caption. Best Value badge: Electric Blue filled pill. Fastest badge: Cyan filled pill. Selected card: Electric Blue 2px border.

Arrival Survey Screen
Bottom sheet, white, 24px radius. Header: place name + 'How was it?' in H2. Questions: emoji scale (5 emoji, evenly spaced, 48px tap targets), binary chips, or single-choice chips. Progress dots at top centre — Electric Blue active, Cloud inactive. Skip button: top-right, Stone text. Completion: animated checkmark in Mint + 'Thanks! We'll remember this' in Body Default.

Social / Friends Screen
Three sub-tabs: Feed, Friends, Plans. Feed cards: friend avatar (40px circle), place photo (56px square), friend name in Body Bold, one-line quote in Body Default Stone, timestamp in Caption. Emoji reaction row: 6 emojis, 36px touch targets. Friend list entries: avatar + name + mutual places count chip. Active location sharing shows green pulse dot on avatar.
11  Accessibility Standards
Mapai targets WCAG 2.1 AA compliance at minimum. Every design decision must be made with accessibility in mind from the start.
